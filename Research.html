Research

For this coursework, I've taken references from a couple of websites since this was my first project.And below i'll try my best to compare and contrast both my website and the websites from where i've taken references.

From Samsung

When i was thinking of ideas as of how my mobile selling e-commerce website should look, i thought why not take advice from the master a.k.a. the biggest mobile phone selling company in the whole world, samsung. And so i did, and i was expecting something flashy and hardcore, i used to think to myself that a company like samsung must spend millions if not billions on their website given that is the one platform that represents their entierity. But i was shocked to see that their website wasa fairly simple and it's there that i realised that too much show and funkiness can be offputting. And when people really wanna buy stuff, they don't look for a website packed with world's best colors and stuff and best pictures, they just want an easily navigatable website. So, it was then that i decided to make my design fairly simple as well.
